About Us

JoRac Hotel is home away from home. Conveniently located approximately 50m off the Embu-Kivaa highway.

This Marvelous Hotel is located near Kiritiri Market, just a 10-minute walk from the Busy developing town and very close to the road. Built in these modern times, it has many rooms which still feature high, crown-molded ceilings. All guestrooms come equipped with free Wi-Fi and all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay. You will absolutely enjoy our services and convenience.

Our rooms are moderately furnished and some have balconies overlooking the serene river that flows adjacent to the hotel. Each guestroom is self-contained with the company of a Television set connected to the current trending movies. You will absolutely enjoy the stay.

Guests are free place their orders in the kitchen and relax in the waiting lounge for the deliveries. As the market is a 10-minute walk from the hotel, visitors are free to enjoy the fresh supplies from the region at the incredibly low prices.

Our Rates are per unit to a specified number of people and not per person/head making this extremely affordable for families and or groups.
